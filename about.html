About Victoria's Mystery

While our names are similiar, this is not a lingerie store. The name simply comes from the site's creator, Victoria Fischer. Victoria is a 20 year old college junior. She studies computer science at the College of Staten Island. She created this e-commerce website to provide customers with their basic needs, along with some fun items they may desire.

Why shop with us? Our products are all USA made, and for every dollar the company makes in profit, we match a dollar to donate to charity. So, by shopping with Victoria's Mystery, you're helping the American economy and those in need! What's better than that?

And no, before you ask, we will never reveal Victoria's Mystery.
